The Pitch

How to Run the "Magic" Demo

Follow these steps to showcase the real-time, multi-role synchronization to your client.

1

Setup the Stage

Open customer.html, vendor.html, rider.html, and admin.html in 4 separate browser tabs. Arrange them side-by-side on your screen.

2

Place the Order

In the Customer App, add items to the cart, proceed to checkout, and click "Place Order". Watch the state initialize.

3

Vendor Fulfillment

Instantly, the Vendor Panel receives a notification. Click "Accept", then "Prep", and finally "Mark as Packed".

4

Rider Dispatch

The moment it's packed, the Rider App triggers a high-priority delivery request with a countdown timer. Accept it to start the map simulation.

5

Admin Oversight

Throughout this entire flow, watch the Admin OS. The GMV ticks up, the Live Orders counter updates, and the AI Command Center logs the activity.

6

Close the Deal

"This is a fully synchronized, multi-role operating system. I can build this exact architecture in Flutter/React Native. When can we kick off?"
